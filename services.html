01. What services does Country Marriott Hospitality Services offer?

We provide comprehensive logistics, hospitality management, and supply chain solutions tailored to meet the needs of our diverse clients. Our services include warehousing, procurement, catering, facility management, and offshore/onshore support services.

02.Where is your company headquartered?

Our headquarters is located in Nigeria, with operational capacity and logistical reach across West Africa and beyond. We also operate regional facilities to serve clients efficiently.

03.Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we do. Our team is trained to manage both local and international projects. We have the infrastructure and global partnerships necessary to support clients outside Nigeria as well.

04.Do you comply with HSE (Health, Safety, Environment) standards?

100%. We adhere strictly to international HSE standards in all our operations. Our team is continuously trained, audited, and certified to maintain safety and environmental responsibility at all times.

05.What industries do you serve?

We primarily serve the oil & gas, maritime, construction, government, and hospitality industries. However, our flexible capabilities allow us to work with clients across multiple sectors.
