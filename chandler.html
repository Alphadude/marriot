Chandler Services

  • Home
  • Chandler Services

In the maritime and offshore world, smooth operations depend on reliable support behind the scenes.

From timely supply deliveries to essential logistics, a professional ship chandler ensures that vessels are equipped, compliant, and ready for their next journey. At Country Marriott Hospitality Services (CMHS), we provide end-to-end chandler services that keep your fleet fully stocked, operational, and compliant with international standards.

Our expertise extends across ports, offshore sites, and inland waterways, guaranteeing efficiency, transparency, and peace of mind. Whether your vessel needs provisions, spare parts, or technical supplies, our team delivers quality products and services on time—every time.

Why Choose Our Chandler Services?

  • Reliability You Can Trust: We understand the urgency of maritime timelines, which is why we prioritize punctual deliveries and dependable support.
  • Tailored to Your Vessel’s Needs: No two vessels are the same. We customize supplies and services to fit your specific operations.
  • Global Standards, Local Knowledge: Our team combines international best practices with a deep understanding of local ports and regulations.
  • Cost-Effective Solutions: We source competitively without compromising on quality, ensuring maximum value for your business.

Key Services & Benefits

1. Provisions & Supplies

From fresh and dry food to hygiene essentials, we ensure your crew is well-catered for with high-quality, safe, and carefully sourced provisions. Our robust supply chain ensures freshness and availability even in remote locations.

Key Benefits:
  • Healthy and satisfied crew members
  • Seamless provisioning with zero downtime
  • Consistent quality across every order.

2. Technical & Deck Stores

We provide a wide range of technical equipment, spare parts, safety gear, and deck stores to support vessel operations. Our catalog includes everything from engine room spares to cleaning materials, ropes, and tools.

Key Benefits:
  • Access to reliable, certified equipment
  • Reduced risk of operational delays
  • On-demand availability of critical part.

3. Logistics & Customs Clearance

Navigating customs regulations and port procedures can be challenging. Our team manages the end-to-end process, ensuring fast, compliant, and hassle-free clearance for all supplies and shipments.

Key Benefits:
  • Reduced administrative burden for your crew
  • Faster turnaround times at ports
  • Compliance with international and local maritime laws.

4. Emergency Support & Rapid Response

Unexpected issues arise at sea. Our rapid-response team is equipped to deliver urgent supplies and assistance, ensuring your vessel remains operational even under pressure.

Key Benefits:
  • Minimized downtime during unforeseen challenges
  • Reliable 24/7 emergency support
  • Confidence knowing help is always a call away.

Our Process:

  • Needs Assessment – We discuss your vessel’s supply and operational requirements.
  • Planning & Coordination – We map out delivery schedules, documentation, and logistics.
  • Supply & Delivery – We source, package, and deliver quality supplies on time.
  • Monitoring & Support – We stay connected to ensure seamless operations and provide ongoing support.

Who We Work With:

Our chandler services are designed for:

  • Commercial Vessels needing reliable, large-scale supply chains
  • Offshore Platforms requiring technical support and emergency provisioning
  • Cargo & Tanker Operators focused on efficiency and compliance.
  • Passenger Vessels & Cruise Ships prioritizing quality, comfort, and safety.

Ready to Partner with a Trusted Chandler?

With years of expertise and a strong reputation for reliability, Country Marriott Hospitality Services (CMHS) is your trusted partner in keeping vessels operational, compliant, and well-supplied. Our solutions go beyond delivering goods—we deliver confidence, continuity, and peace of mind.
